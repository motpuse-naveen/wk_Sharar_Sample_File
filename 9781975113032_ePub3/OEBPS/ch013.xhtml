Cardiovascular Pharmacology

Paul S. Pagel, Justin N. Tawil, Brent T. Boettcher, and Julie K. Freed

This chapter reviews the cardiovascular pharmacology of medications used to alter hemodynamics during surgery and in the intensive care unit, including endogenous and synthetic catecholamines, sympathomimetics, milrinone, vasopressin, and antihypertensive medications.
